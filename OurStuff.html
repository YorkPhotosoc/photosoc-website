Darkroom

The darkroom is located in the biology building, and can be used to develop a variety of film. It comes fully stocked with a variety of chemicals, all of which are free to use with your membership. To gain acess to the darkroom, you must first complete on of our darkroom training sessions, which are run throughout the year. Once this is completed, you can freely book the darkroom, allowing you to use any of the equipment inside. Upon completing this training, you will be emailed a link to be able to book out the darkroom.

Studio

Our studio is located in the biology building, in room fjadslk. They key can be loaned out from Vanbrugh Reception, as long as you are a member of the society. It is fully kitted out with a selection of backgrounds, lighting and tripods. Members can book out the studio during term time for all purposes by simply putting it on the shared google calender.
