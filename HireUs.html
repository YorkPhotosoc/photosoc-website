Our Services

Our society contians a plethora of talented photographers that you can hire out to photograph your events through us


How it works

To hire out one of our photographers, first you must complete our show of interest form that can be found here Upon completing this form, the job will be advertised to our members through our website and Instagram. Those who are interested will submit their portfolio, which will then be forwarded onto you. From there, you will be able to pick the photographer you would like to hire, and we will put them in touch with you.
